Kinnihuman Truetype Font for Windows (version 1)

2020 Iconian Fonts - Daniel Zadorozny

This font comes in two variations: Regular and 3D.

http://www.iconian.com

This is a dingbat font with over 90 glyphs of those little, rubber toy monsters that were big back in the 80's.

Use of a character map program, such as Windows' Character Map, will be very helpful in exploring and using this font.
